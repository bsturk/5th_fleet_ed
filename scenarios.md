### **Scenario 1: The Battle of the Maldives**
*   **Game Length:** 5 turns (40 hours)
*   **Complexity:** Low
*   **Forces: Green Player:**
    *   One Marine Corps AV-8B (Harrier) squadron and a US Navy P-3C squadron deployed on Male Atoll.
    *   Two additional P-3C squadrons are situated at Diego Garcia.
    *   A four-ship surface action group, including the battleship USS *Missouri*, is in the Maldives zone.
    *   A fast convoy of four SL-7 containerships, escorted by four frigates, is located in the West Indian Ocean zone.
    *   A single US submarine, the USS *Honolulu*, is supporting the surface action group.
*   **Forces: Red Player:**
    *   Several air reconnaissance squadrons of the Indian Air Force and Navy are situated in southern India.
    *   Two combat squadrons, including one trained in naval attack, are based at Trivandrum airfield.
    *   Both of the Indian Navy's aircraft carriers, escorted by four destroyers and frigates, are in position off the southwestern Indian coast.
    *   Four Indian Navy destroyers of the *Modified Kashin* class are in the Laccadives zone, sailing towards the Maldives.
    *   Two submarines are supporting the surface forces.
*   **Objectives: Green Player:**
    *   The fast convoy (FC) units *Antares* and *Capella* must reach Male Atoll.
    *   Destroy as many Indian units as possible.
*   **Objectives: Red Player:**
    *   Destroy or damage the US airfield on Male Atoll.
    *   Destroy as many US units as possible.
*   **Special Note:**
    *   The green player may not make cruise missile attacks in this scenario.

### **Scenario 2: Russian Raiders**
*   **Game Length:** 12 turns (4 days)
*   **Complexity:** Low
*   **Forces: Green Player:**
    *   Several unescorted convoys (supertankers, freighters, fast container-ships) are situated in the western Indian Ocean.
    *   Three US Navy surface action groups are sailing to the aid of the convoys.
    *   One group of four frigates, known as the "Middle East Force," is replenishing in Raysut, Oman.
    *   Another group of five surface combatants from the 7th Fleet is currently located in the West Indian Ocean zone.
    *   A powerful group of five warships, including the battleship USS *New Jersey*, is sailing off the coast of Kenya.
    *   Two submarines are currently deployed to the region.
    *   One squadron of P-3C reconnaissance aircraft at Diego Garcia is supporting the operation.
*   **Forces: Red Player:**
    *   A four-ship task force, including the nuclear-powered cruiser *Nakhimov*, is somewhere in the vicinity of the Seychelles.
    *   An *Oscar*-class submarine is currently in the Arabian Sea.
    *   Three air reconnaissance squadrons are deployed to airfields in Yemen and on Socotra island.
*   **Objective: Green Player:**
    *   Destroy as many Russian units as possible.
*   **Objectives: Red Player:**
    *   The four Russian surface warships must reach Aden, Al Mukalla, or Ras Karma while destroying as many US merchant ships and warships as possible.

### **Scenario 3: Battle of the Arabian Sea**
*   **Game Length:** 10 turns (80 hours)
*   **Complexity:** Low
*   **Forces: Green Player:**
    *   A French carrier battle group is situated north of the Seychelles.
    *   American and British carrier groups are situated in the Gulf of Oman and North Arabian Sea.
    *   Three US Navy submarines have been deployed to the Arabian Sea.
    *   A French Mirage squadron is deployed at Djibouti.
    *   US Navy P-3C reconnaissance squadrons are stationed at Diego Garcia, Mombasa (in Kenya), and Masirah (in Oman).
*   **Forces: Red Player:**
    *   A Russian battle group is operating in the western end of the Gulf of Aden.
    *   Two other Russian Navy carrier battle groups are deployed in the vicinity of Socotra island.
    *   Six Russian submarines are acting as supports for the surface forces.
    *   Russian Navy strike, electronic warfare, and reconnaissance aircraft are deployed at airfields in Al Mukalla and Aden as well as on the island of Socotra.
    *   Russian and Yemeni Air Force fighter squadrons are also deployed at these airfields.
*   **Objectives: Green Player:**
    *   Destroy as many Russian units as possible.
    *   Destroy the Russian airfields at Ras Karma, Aden, and Al Mukalla.
*   **Objectives: Red Player:**
    *   Destroy as many US, UK, and French units as possible.
    *   Russian surface and submarine units must occupy the Gulf of Oman zone at the end of the scenario. Failing that, they must occupy either the North Arabian Sea or South Arabian Sea zones.

### **Scenario 4: Carrier Raid**
*   **Game Length:** 12 turns (4 days)
*   **Complexity:** Low
*   **Forces: Green Player:**
    *   A single US Navy carrier battle group is located somewhere in the western Indian Ocean.
    *   The group is supported by a single submarine.
    *   P-3C reconnaissance squadrons in Diego Garcia, Mombasa (in Kenya), and Victoria (in the Seychelles) are supporting the operation.
*   **Forces: Red Player:**
    *   Russian and Yemeni combat and support aircraft are deployed at airfields in Aden, Al Mukalla, Berbera, Mogadishu, and Socotra island.
    *   Three small Russian convoys, each consisting of three supply ships and two destroyers, are sailing for Socotra.
    *   Three additional Russian surface warships, supported by five submarines, are patrolling the waters around Socotra.
*   **Objectives: Green Player:**
    *   Destroy the Russian airfields at Aden, Berbera, Mogadishu, Al Mukalla, and Ras Karma.
    *   Destroy as many Russian and Yemeni units as possible.
*   **Objectives: Red Player:**
    *   Russian oilers (AO), ammunition ships (AE), and supply ships (AOR) must attempt to reach Ras Karma on Socotra island.
    *   Destroy as many US units as possible.

### **Scenario 5: Locate and Destroy**
*   **Game Length:** 9 turns (3 days)
*   **Complexity:** Low
*   **Forces: Green Player:**
    *   A single US Navy carrier battle group has been deployed to the western Indian Ocean, supported by two submarines.
    *   A squadron of P-3C reconnaissance aircraft is deployed at Diego Garcia.
*   **Forces: Red Player:**
    *   The *Ushakov*, accompanied by the new Russian aircraft carrier *Varyag*, is situated somewhere northeast of Socotra Island.
    *   Two Russian destroyers are currently sailing near Aden, and two more are in the Strait of Hormuz.
    *   Four Indian destroyers are also ready to escort the *Ushakov*.
    *   A single Russian submarine is currently on station in the Arabian Sea.
    *   Indian Air Force reconnaissance aircraft are patrolling the Indian Ocean.
    *   A Russian Navy reconnaissance squadron is deployed at Bandar 'Abbas, Iran.
    *   A Tu-26 Backfire regiment is deployed at Aden, supported by fighters and reconnaissance aircraft.
*   **Objective: Green Player:**
    *   Sink the *Ushakov*.
*   **Objective: Red Player:**
    *   The *Ushakov* must reach Aden, Bandar 'Abbas, Bombay, or Cochin.
*   **Special Note:**
    *   Neither player may make cruise missile attacks in this scenario.

### **Scenario 6: Convoy Battles**
*   **Game Length:** 7 turns (56 hours)
*   **Complexity:** Medium
*   **Forces: Green Player:**
    *   Several American supertankers are heading for the Strait of Malacca.
    *   Four large convoys of merchant ships and US Navy supply vessels are sailing for Diego Garcia.
    *   Six surface action groups, including one from the Royal Australian Navy, are in position to escort these convoys.
    *   Two US Navy submarines are also on station within the region.
    *   Two US Navy P-3C reconnaissance squadrons are deployed to Diego Garcia.
    *   An Australian P-3C squadron is stationed at Banda Aceh airfield.
*   **Forces: Red Player:**
    *   Eight Russian submarines are situated in the theater.
    *   Three Russian Navy destroyers are replenishing at Colombo in Sri Lanka, with a naval infantry battalion embarked.
    *   A MiG-23 fighter regiment, supported by a Russian Navy reconnaissance squadron, is currently deployed at Addu Atoll.
    *   Another air reconnaissance squadron is at Male Atoll.
    *   Two additional reconnaissance squadrons are stationed in Sri Lanka.
    *   A Tu-95D "Bear" reconnaissance squadron is deployed at the small Indian airfield at Port Blair.
*   **Objectives: Green Player:**
    *   US slow convoys (SC), fast convoys (FC), oilers (AO), ammunition ships (AE), and supply ships (AOR) must reach Diego Garcia.
    *   US full tankers (FT) must reach the Strait of Malacca.
    *   Destroy as many Russian units as possible.
*   **Objectives: Red Player:**
    *   The three Russian destroyers (DD/DDG) must reach Addu Atoll.
    *   Destroy as many US and Australian units as possible.

### **Scenario 7: Action in the Bay of Bengal**
*   **Game Length:** 9 turns (3 days)
*   **Complexity:** Medium
*   **Forces: Green Player:**
    *   One US Navy carrier battle group is currently southeast of Sri Lanka. Another carrier battle group is located southwest of Sumatra.
    *   An Indonesian surface battle group is operating in the western approaches to the Strait of Malacca.
    *   An Australian task force is currently situated south of Sumatra.
    *   One Royal Australian Navy and three US Navy submarines are currently deployed to the region.
    *   A P-3C reconnaissance squadron is stationed at the US base on Diego Garcia.
    *   At the Banda Aceh airfield are two additional P-3C squadrons (including an Australian squadron) and three combat squadrons from a US Marine Corps air wing.
*   **Forces: Red Player:**
    *   A large Indian Navy task force, including two aircraft carriers, has just departed the navy base at Vishakapatnam.
    *   A Russian Navy carrier group from the Pacific fleet is currently situated near the Andaman Islands.
    *   A second Russian carrier battle group from the Northern fleet is located north of Sri Lanka.
    *   Five Russian Navy submarines are on station in the theater.
    *   Russian long-range strike aircraft, supported by fighter, electronic warfare, and reconnaissance units, are deployed to airfields at Calcutta, Yangon (Rangoon), Car Nicobar, and Port Blair.
*   **Objectives: Green Player:**
    *   Destroy the Russian airfields at Port Blair and Car Nicobar.
    *   The US CVN *Lincoln* and the CV *America* must end the game within bombing range (of an F/A-18E) of Port Blair.
    *   Destroy as many Russian and Indian units as possible.
*   **Objective: Red Player:**
    *   Destroy as many US, Indonesian, and Australian units as possible.

### **Scenario 8: Convoys to Iran**
*   **Game Length:** 15 turns (5 days)
*   **Complexity:** Medium
*   **Forces: Green Player:**
    *   A US Navy carrier battle group is situated about 700 miles northeast of the Seychelles.
    *   A fast convoy of SL-7 container-ships, escorted by four frigates, is sailing northward about 200 miles east of the coast of Somalia.
    *   A convoy of MPS ships, escorted by four Royal Navy warships, is located off the horn of Africa.
    *   A second MPS convoy, escorted by two US Navy frigates, is about 250 miles west of Male Atoll.
    *   Two US Navy amphibious ready groups are entering the southern portion of the Arabian Sea.
    *   Two US Navy submarines are on station in the Indian Ocean.
    *   Two squadrons of US Navy minesweepers are in the Gulf of Oman and the Persian Gulf.
    *   At Masirah airfield in Oman is a composite US Air Force wing (F-16C and F-15E) supported by a US Navy P-3C reconnaissance squadron.
    *   At Dubai are elements of a US Marine Corps air wing supported by a second P-3C squadron.
*   **Forces: Red Player:**
    *   Two powerful Russian carrier battle groups are in position; one is in the northern part of the Arabian Sea, a second is located near Socotra island.
    *   Six Russian submarines are also in the theater.
    *   Long-range Russian strike aircraft, supported by fighter, reconnaissance and electronic warfare units, are deployed at airfields in Aden (in Yemen), Ras Karma (on Socotra island), and Yazd (in Iran).
    *   A single reconnaissance squadron is located at Al Mukalla airfield in Yemen.
*   **Objectives: Green Player:**
    *   US fast convoy (FC), maritime prepositioning (MPS), and all amphibious assault units (LHD, LPD, LSD, and LHA) must reach Bandar 'Abbas.
    *   Failing that, they must attempt to reach Masirah (in Oman), Muscat (in the United Arab Emirates), or Jiwani (in Pakistan).
    *   Destroy as many Russian units as possible.
*   **Objective: Red Player:**
    *   Destroy as many US and British units as possible.

### **Scenario 9: Indian Ocean Sideshow**
*   **Game Length:** 15 turns (5 days)
*   **Complexity:** High
*   **Forces: Green Player:**
    *   A large US Navy carrier battle group is escorting an amphibious ready group in the Arabian Sea.
    *   A surface action group, including the refurbished battleship USS *Missouri*, is located about 600 miles northeast of Diego Garcia.
    *   Four submarines are deployed in the theater.
    *   Several convoys of tankers, maritime prepositioning ships, freighters, and US Navy supply ships are scattered over the region, now joined by many US Navy frigates.
    *   Two squadrons of American minesweepers are in and near the Persian Gulf.
    *   Four US Navy P-3C reconnaissance squadrons: two at Diego Garcia, one at Masirah, and one in Mombasa.
    *   A B-52G squadron is deployed to Diego Garcia.
    *   An Air Force composite wing (including F-22A fighters) and an F-15C squadron are at Al Muharraq airfield in Bahrain and Abu Dhabi.
    *   Elements of a US Marine Corps air wing are stationed at the Salalah airfield.
    *   Another Marine fighter squadron and an Air Force F-117A Stealth fighter squadron are in Muscat.
    *   Another US Air Force composite wing is at the Banda Aceh airfield.
*   **Forces: Red Player:**
    *   A Russian carrier battle group is operating in the vicinity of Socotra island, and a second carrier group is situated in the Bay of Bengal.
    *   A task force of four destroyers and frigates is sailing in the Gulf of Aden.
    *   An oiler and ammunition ship have just arrived at Car Nicobar.
    *   Ten submarines are dispersed throughout the Indian Ocean.
    *   Several squadrons of long-range attack aircraft, fighters, reconnaissance, and electronic warfare aircraft are operating from airfields in Yemen, including Ras Karma on Socotra island.
    *   Russian air units have permission to operate from Iranian airfields, with long-range attack and reconnaissance aircraft (including Navy Tu-26 Backfires) deployed to Shiraz, Yazd, and Zahedan.
    *   In the eastern Indian Ocean, one reconnaissance and two fighter squadrons are operating from the Indian airfield at Car Nicobar.
    *   Three more squadrons are based at the Yangon (Rangoon) international airport.
*   **Objectives: Green Player:**
    *   All US amphibious assault units (LHD, LPD, LSD, but not the LHA *Tarawa*) and maritime prepositioning ships (MPS) must reach Socotra island. Failing that, they must reach Raysut or Salalah.
    *   US full tanker units (FT) must reach the Strait of Malacca.
    *   US empty tanker (ET) units must reach Kuwait, Ras al Mishab, Al Jubayl, Ras Tannurah, or Al Manamah.
    *   US fast convoy (FC) and slow convoy (SC) units must reach Diego Garcia.
    *   Destroy as many Russian units as possible.
*   **Objective: Red Player:**
    *   Destroy as many US units as possible.
*   **Special Notes:**
    *   US surface and submarine units may only perform in-port replenishment in Al Manamah (Bahrain), Muscat (Oman), Diego Garcia, and Mombasa (Kenya).
    *   Russian surface and submarine units may only perform in-port replenishment in Massawa (Ethiopia), Aden (Yemen), Ras Karma (Socotra island), and Vishakapatnam (India).

### **Scenario 10: The Indian Ocean War**
*   **Game Length:** 30 turns (10 days)
*   **Complexity:** Very High
*   **Forces: Green Player:**
    *   One Royal Navy, one French Navy, and three US Navy carrier battle groups are in the Indian Ocean.
    *   The US Navy's *Lincoln* carrier group, with battleship USS *Missouri*, is escorting an amphibious ready group (ARG) in the Gulf of Oman.
    *   The *Washington* carrier group is escorting another ARG in the eastern Indian Ocean.
    *   A third ARG is at Diego Garcia with an assortment of US Navy frigates, destroyers, maritime prepositioning ships, an ammunition ship, and an oiler.
    *   At Diego Garcia is a combined US Air Force-US Navy air wing (B-52G, F-15C, and two P-3C squadrons).
    *   The navies and air forces of Saudi Arabia, Kuwait, Bahrain, Qatar, Oman, Pakistan, and the United Arab Emirates are actively supporting.
    *   Allied airfields are established at Victoria (Seychelles), Mombasa (Kenya), and at Djibouti.
    *   A US Marine Corps air wing and a US Air Force fighter squadron are deployed at Bandar 'Abbas, Iran.
    *   At Bandar-e Lengeh are two US Navy supply ships, a minesweeper group, and two frigates.
    *   Several escorted and unescorted convoys are scattered over the region.
    *   The allies are using the Banda Aceh airfield, where a US Air Force composite fighter wing and American and Australian P-3C squadrons are deployed.
    *   A US Navy underway replenishment group (URG) is off the western tip of Sumatra.
    *   Fifteen American, British, French, Australian, and Pakistani submarines are on patrol.
*   **Forces: Red Player:**
    *   A Russian carrier battle group is deployed near Socotra island. A second is in the Bay of Bengal, supported from Trincomalee, Sri Lanka.
    *   The Indian Navy has committed both of its carrier groups.
    *   Indian Navy smaller surface combatants are stationed at Karwar and Vishakapatnam.
    *   A flotilla of Russian Navy minesweepers, frigates, and supply ships is docked at Aden.
    *   Several Indian Air Force combat and reconnaissance squadrons have deployed to southern India and Male Atoll.
    *   Yemen, Ethiopia, and Iran have offered the services of their air forces and navies.
    *   The Russians have established major airfields at Aden, Al Mukalla, and on Socotra island.
    *   The Russians have deployed several long-range attack, fighter, and support squadrons to Esfahan, Yazd, Zahedan, and Kerman in Iran.
    *   Russian aircraft have permission to operate from airfields in the Andaman and Nicobar Islands.
    *   The Russians have also deployed an air wing to Yangon (Rangoon), Burma.
    *   Twenty-six Russian, Indian, and Iranian submarines are scattered over the region.
*   **Objectives: Green Player:**
    *   All US amphibious assault units and maritime prepositioning ships must reach any of the following ports: Bandar 'Abbas (Iran), Bandar-e Lengeh (Iran), Karachi (Pakistan), Port Blair (Adaman Islands), or Car Nicobar (Nicobar Islands). Failing that, they must reach Muscat (Oman) and/or Banda Aceh (Sumatra).
    *   US full tanker (FT) units must reach the Strait of Malacca.
    *   US empty tanker (ET) units must reach Kuwait, Ras al Mishab, Al Jubayl, Ras Tannurah, and/or Al Manamah.
    *   US fast convoy (FC) and slow convoy (SC) units must reach Diego Garcia.
    *   Destroy as many enemy units as possible.
*   **Objective: Red Player:**
    *   Destroy as many enemy units as possible.
*   **Special Notes:**
    *   Green player units may only perform in-port replenishment in Kuwait, Al Jubayl (Saudi Arabia), Al Manamah (Bahrain), Muscat (Oman), Diego Garcia, Djibouti, and Mombasa (Kenya).
    *   Red player units may only perform in-port replenishment in Massawa (Ethiopia), Aden (Yemen), Ras Karma (Socotra island), Vishakapatnam, Madras, Bombay, and Cochin (all on India's coast).
